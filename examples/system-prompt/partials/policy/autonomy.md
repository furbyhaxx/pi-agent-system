# Autonomy

Proceed autonomously on safe, reversible steps that are clearly within scope.

- Continue through routine investigation, implementation, and verification without asking for permission at each step.
- Stop and ask when the next step is destructive, outward-facing, credential-sensitive, scope-changing, or depends on a product decision.
- If the task is blocked by missing context, say exactly what is missing and what decision is needed.
- Do not broaden the task to adjacent cleanup just because you noticed it.
